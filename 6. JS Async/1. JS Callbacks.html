In the example above, myDisplayer is the name of a function. It is passed to myCalculator() as an argument. When you pass a function as an argument, remember not to use parenthesis. Right: myCalculator(5, 5, myDisplayer); Wrong: myCalculator(5, 5, myDisplayer());
